SPYDLE

Daily spy cipher puzzle game

🕵️What is Spydle?

Spydle is a free daily puzzle game where you decode intercepted spy messages, one word at a time. Each day brings a new classified mission with a hidden message to crack.

Solve crossword-style clues to reveal each word of the message. Get green, yellow, and gray feedback on each guess — just like Wordle, but with a spy twist.

🎮How to play

Each mission contains a secret message broken into individual words. You receive a clue for each word and type your answer. Letters turn green if correct, yellow if they're in the word but wrong position, and gray if not in the word at all.

You can tackle the words in any order — tap a word in the message bar to switch. Solve all words to complete the mission. A new puzzle drops every day at midnight US Eastern time.

After completing the daily mission, watch a short promo to unlock a bonus mission!

🔊Sound & Music

Spydle features typing sound effects and an original spy-lofi background track. Music starts automatically on your first interaction — tap the ♪ button to toggle it off.
